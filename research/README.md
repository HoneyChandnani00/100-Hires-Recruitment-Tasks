## What This Project Is

This folder contains primary research into how B2B SaaS companies and founders use LinkedIn organic content to build audiences, generate pipeline, and drive revenue without relying on paid advertising.

10 high-signal practitioners were identified, their recent content collected, and everything organised into a structured research repository strong enough to support a full LinkedIn content playbook.

---

## Why I Chose This Topic

LinkedIn organic content for B2B SaaS sits at the intersection of two powerful trends:

1. Founder-led growth is replacing traditional outbound sales as the primary pipeline driver for early-stage SaaS companies
2. LinkedIn is becoming the number one organic channel for B2B audiences, outperforming blogs, Twitter/X, and email for reach and pipeline generation

Understanding how the best practitioners actually do this in practice is the foundation of any effective B2B SaaS go-to-market strategy in 2026.

## How Content Was Collected

### LinkedIn Posts
- Collected manually from each expert's LinkedIn profile
- 2 posts per expert selected for tactical relevance
- Each post saved with full text, URL, date, and analysis note
- Selection criteria: teaching a tactic or framework, not promoting products

### YouTube Transcripts
- Collected automatically using the Supadata API
- PowerShell script used to pull clean transcript text directly into the repo
- 8 transcripts collected covering LinkedIn strategy, algorithm insights, content systems, and B2B growth frameworks

### Additional Resources
- Rosanna Campbell's B2B Writing Club newsletter on Substack
- Richard van der Blom's LinkedIn Algorithm Report 2026
