## Post Content

LinkedIn ghostwriting teaches you things you can't learn from writing
your own posts.

One of my biggest takeaways:
We worry way too much about repeating ourselves.

Your audience has seen maybe 10% of what you've posted.
You've seen 100% of it.

The repetition that feels embarrassing to you is completely invisible to them.

So if you're sitting on a post because you feel like you've said it before —
I guarantee that nobody will notice.
And even if they do, you'll say it better this time.
