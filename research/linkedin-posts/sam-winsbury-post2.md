## Post Content

Let me make this painfully simple:
Selling is 10x easier when more people:

1. know you
2. like you
3. trust you

A personal brand is just a system to do that at scale.

If you're starting from scratch, do this:

1. Get your positioning right
Don't post until you can clearly answer:
a. who you help
b. what result you drive
c. why you're credible
d. what makes you different

If you can't answer that, your content will never convert.

2. Turn content into a 24/7 salesperson
Most people treat content as "just post".
Wrong. Content is distribution of your thinking.

It should:
a. reach new people (growth)
b. build trust (authority)
c. drive action (conversion)

Miss one? You don't get results.

3. Bridge the commitment gap
This is the bit nobody talks about.

People don't go: See post → buy $3,000+ service

You need steps:
content → profile → lead magnet → sales mechanism → call

Each one nudges them closer to buying.

→ Visibility without positioning is noise.
→ Credibility without amplification is invisible.
→ Attention without conversion is wasted.
