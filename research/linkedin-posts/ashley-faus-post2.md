## Post Content

The number one thing you MUST do on LinkedIn if you want to reap the benefits:
COMMENT!

Comments continue to pop off on LinkedIn.
Some of them get more reach than an original post!

Here's the mindset shift:

🗣️ Comments = Conversation
Most guru advice around comments is to chase big creators to draft off their reach.
But leaving a wimpy "Great post! Agree! Love this!" reply is not gonna get you the benefits!
Instead, comments should start or continue conversations.
You wouldn't walk around an in-person event just popping into conversations with 1-2 platitudes — so don't do that online.

💡 Comments = Creativity
Ever struggled to know what to post, with the blank box mocking you?
Lots of other people have already said something, so you can just react to their thoughts!

Prompts as you engage in the feed:
→ I agree with this because...
→ I disagree with this because...
→ An example of this from my work...

✍️ Comments = Content
A meaty comment should turn into a standalone post.
When you add a unique perspective and examples, you're giving enough value for people to dig in and converse.

How to do it:
Screenshot the original post → use your comment as the body of the new post → at-mention the OP to give credit and drive engagement.

Results from one example: 16k impressions and counting, banter in the replies, and an additional standalone post.
