## Post Content

Reach should never be your goal. Nor engagement.
The ultimate goal? A DM conversation with your ICP.

Most B2B marketers are still measuring LinkedIn the way they measured it
3 years ago — impressions, follower count growth, and post likes.
None of those metrics correlate with pipeline.

Here's what actually does:

TOFU — aim for Awareness
Track saves from non-followers and profile visits.
A save from a stranger is the clearest early signal of future intent.

MOFU — aim for Credibility
Track comment quality, newsletter subscribers, and return profile visits
within 7–30 days.
Saves on credibility content are the #1 trigger for inbound DMs.

BOFU — aim for Conversion
Track DMs initiated, meetings booked, Lead Gen Form completions.
LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms convert at 10–13% vs 2–4% for typical landing pages.

The DM is the deal.
Average DM response rate: 10.3%
Cold email response rate: 3.1%
Almost 3x better.

The single metric that matters most isn't reach.
It's the ratio of qualified DM conversations to total impressions.

Everything else — format choices, content pillars, posting frequency —
exists in service of that one ratio.
