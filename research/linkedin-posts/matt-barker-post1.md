## Post Content

I've written 2,600+ LinkedIn posts. But the real skill isn't writing, it's:
Psychology.

Every question based CTA I write is either:

1. A yes / no question. Easy answer + low barrier to engage.
2. A question nobody's meant to answer. It just lingers.

e.g. "How much longer will you put off writing online?"

I don't want comments. I want them to think about it in the shower or when they're doing a poo.

Most people write CTAs to get likes but I write them to get into your head.
