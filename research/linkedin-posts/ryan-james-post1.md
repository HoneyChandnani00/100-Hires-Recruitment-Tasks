## Post Content

The easiest way to get leads from LinkedIn:

1. Fire up a LinkedIn poll
2. Ask people what their goals/challenges are, all related to your services
3. Check the voters' LinkedIn profiles. Qualify them.
4. DM the ones who pass qualification:

"Thanks for voting on my poll! I see you're struggling with [problem].
We helped [company] solve that with [your service].
They got [result].
Fancy jumping on a call tomorrow morning, where I can explain more?"
