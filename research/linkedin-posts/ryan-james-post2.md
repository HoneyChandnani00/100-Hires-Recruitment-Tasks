## Post Content

The biggest startup marketing mistake is a lack of consistency in content.

🐌 Slow growth = doing content when you have the time
🏎️ Rapid growth = A consistent rhythm of great content

## Why This Post Was Selected

Ryan distils the single biggest failure point in B2B SaaS content marketing
into one clear contrast — sporadic content vs consistent rhythm. For early
stage SaaS founders, this is the most important strategic principle before
any tactical framework: consistency is the prerequisite for LinkedIn organic
growth to work at all. Ryan's perspective carries extra credibility because
it comes from running a SaaS marketing agency (Rocket SaaS) that has grown
from $1M to $7M ARR — meaning this insight is drawn from observing dozens
of SaaS companies succeed and fail at content marketing, not just personal
brand building.
